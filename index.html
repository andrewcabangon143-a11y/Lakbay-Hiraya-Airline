About Lakbay Hiraya Airlines

Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Manila, Lakbay Hiraya Airlines was born from a singular dream — to make every Filipino destination accessible, affordable, and unforgettable. Our name, meaning "Journey of Dreams," captures our mission perfectly.

We operate a modern fleet of Airbus A320s and ATR 72s serving 32 domestic destinations across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao, with an impeccable safety record and award-winning service.

As the first Filipino carrier to achieve IATA operational safety audit certification three consecutive years, we combine world-class standards with the warmth of malasakit — genuine care for every passenger.
